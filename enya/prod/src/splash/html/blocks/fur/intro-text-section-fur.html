Together, we can save them.

Why do people still buy animal fur as a fashion statement? The Humane Society of the United States is attacking this question at every angle—working to stop both consumer supply and demand. We conduct undercover investigations to expose the cruelty of extreme confinement and brutal methods of slaughter on fur farms. Together, with our supporters we’ve convinced major retailers and brands to stop using fur, and are working with governments to ban fur farming and the sale of fur all over the world.

Buying animal fur as a fashion accessory is completely unnecessary when there are so many alternatives that are better for animals and the planet. This is a fight we CAN win, but we need your support. Please, donate now to help.
