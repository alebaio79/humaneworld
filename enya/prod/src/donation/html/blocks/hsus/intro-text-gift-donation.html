Honor a friend, relative or even a pet with a gift donation to the Humane Society of the United States. Gift donations are a great alternative to a traditional birthday, anniversary or holiday gift—you can even send one just to say you care!

For a donation of $19 or more, we’ll mail your recipient a personalized greeting card with your special message.* For a donation of $25 or more, the recipient will also receive a one-year subscription to All Animals, the Humane Society of the United States’ award-winning member magazine.

With your gift donation, you’re helping the Humane Society of the United States fight for all animals. That’s definitely something to celebrate!

If a gift donation isn’t the right fit, you may want to make a memorial donation in remembrance of a friend, pet or other loved one instead.
