Help end cruel and unnecessary animal experiments.

It’s the way it has always been done—but with so many technological advances, why are we still using animals in experiments?

Millions of animals are suffering right now in laboratories across the United States. Dogs who have never had the chance to roll in the grass or fetch a ball are deliberately sickened with toxic chemicals or infected with diseases, live in barren cages, and are typically killed when the experiment ends. Terrified rabbits, guinea pigs, rats and mice have substances dripped into their eyes or forced down their throats to test cosmetics like shampoo and lipstick. No animal deserves to suffer this way.

Humane testing methods, such as human cell-based tests and sophisticated computer modeling, are proving to be faster, more reliable, and less expensive than outdated animal experiments. We are working with scientists, policymakers, and government agencies to reduce and eventually end harmful animal experiments. We need your support to make this important work possible.

The Humane Society of the United States is the nation’s most effective animal protection organization. Together with our supporters, we can end cruel and unnecessary animal experiments. Please donate now to help.
