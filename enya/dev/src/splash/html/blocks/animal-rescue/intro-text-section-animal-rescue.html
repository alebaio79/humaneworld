You can help animals in crisis find relief.

Every year, we rescue scared animals who are suffering alone in situations of cruelty or neglect. Please make an urgent donation to help us provide immediate support to animals in natural disasters, puppy mills, animal fighting operations or other dire emergencies. Your donation helps rescue and protect all animals.

Our highly trained Animal Rescue and Response team is at the heart of this lifesaving work. During natural disasters, we work with local agencies to clear shelters and transport animals who are in harm’s way, secure food and other critical resources, and carry out emergency field rescue for animals who are in danger and in desperate need of help. We manage programs and spearhead efforts designed to ease the burden on local shelter groups during these times of crisis.

In partnership with law enforcement, we investigate and rescue animals from the most heartbreaking cases of abuse and suffering. We also advocate year-round to strengthen cruelty laws which help prevent animal abuse before it happens.

With your help, we can deploy at a moment’s notice to provide relief to animals in desperate need.
